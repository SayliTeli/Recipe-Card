🍍 Pineapple Cake

Prep Time: 30 mins Serves: 6
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • ½ cup crushed pineapple
  • ½ cup yogurt
  • ¼ cup oil
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Whipped cream for topping
  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder.
  3. Add pineapple, yogurt, and oil.
  4. Pour into pan and bake for 25–30 mins.
  5. Cool and top with whipped cream.
